About the Project
Overview

Landing page design for Subscra, a subscription revenue platform that helps creators and businesses convert visitors into paying members. The long-form page walks users through an onboarding flow preview, core feature highlights, a live revenue calculator, competitor comparison table, and a three-tier pricing section. All anchored by bold metric callouts and a dark interface with sharp teal and green neon accents that signal growth and performance.

About the Project
Overview

Social media advertisement designed for SotoGrande Katipunan promoting a Mother's Day "Stay & Dine with Mom" package starting at ₱4,200, valid throughout May 2025. The square-format ad uses a warm, candlelit dining scene as the full-bleed background an elegantly plated meal surrounded by soft bokeh lights that evoke intimacy and celebration. A inset photo of the hotel room is anchored in the lower right, giving viewers a clear glimpse of the accommodation. White script and gold ampersand typography carry the same visual language as the Father's Day counterpart, maintaining brand consistency across the campaign series. Package inclusions breakfast for 2, gym and pool access, and a set lunch or dinner for 2 are listed cleanly at the bottom to inform and drive conversions.
